Nova's ancestry consists of marksmen and snipers. A cool part about Nova's ancestry is that Wolfgang Schreiber is her great ancestor and thus the reason why Nova is more of a speed fighter that hits hard than a tank like her teacher,Himiko.

Like her sensei, Nova is an all range fighter but lacks Himiko's shielding capabilities. To compensate for that flaw however, she makes that up for her skilled hand to hand combat skills.

Icicle Variable Shot:
Gathering ice, Nova forms icicle bullets of multiple layers. Her ice bullets also bypass immunities to ice manipulation forcing her opponent to think twice before melting her ice.

Morgandammerung:
Taking on a seperate orange and sky blue trajectory, Nova can trace and track the sound of her opponent's movements.

Nova unlike her fiery and brash teacher Himiko, she is level headed and even in the heat of battle she maintains her cool.
